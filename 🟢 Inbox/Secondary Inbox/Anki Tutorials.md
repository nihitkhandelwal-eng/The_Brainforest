#### Anki Terminology
- Burying a card
	- makes the card appear tomorrow without disturbing the algorithm
	- suppose you pressed "show" by accident
	- no color
- Suspending a card
	- color → yellow 
- Marking a card
	- it's like starring a card
	- color → purple
